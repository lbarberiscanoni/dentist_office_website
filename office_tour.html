Office Tour

Upstate Pediatric Dentistry is designed to look like the new downtown Greenville and Falls Park. The life-size oak tree and grass floor in the waiting room are inviting to children of all ages. Because the team at Upstate Pediatric Dentistry believes that going to the dentist can be as fun and stress-free as going to the park, our office is designed to make kids (and parents, too) feel as if they are relaxing outside in the sunshine. We can't wait to see you soon.
